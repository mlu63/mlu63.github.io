Michael Lu

"I have no special talent. I am only passionately curious" - Albert Einstein

I am a recent graduate from The University of Western Ontario, with an Honors Specialization in Computer Science and a minor in Software Engieering. I am very passionate about video games, and in my free time I love to either play games or develop them. I have experience both as a casual and competitive gamer as well as a game creator, and in the future I'd like to pursue a related career. You can read more about my skill and qualifications here. I also enjoy frequenting Hackatons and learning more skills!

Apart from gaming, I have a deep interest in all aspects of fitness and nutrition. One of my favourite hobbies is keeping up with the professional bodybuilding scene. You can often find me cooking my meals in advance or working out in the gym; both of these consume a lot of time! One of the best experiences of my life was meeting 4x Mr.Olympia Jay Cutler at the 2014 Toronto Pro Supershow. One of my long-term goals is to step on stage as a competitor, and hopefully to win!
